about the studio

A reservoir-side studio built for real moments, not stiff poses

Tucked beside the Almatti reservoir in Karnataka, we trade studio backdrops for open sky, dusty roads and quiet water. Every shoot is shaped around the two of you — the vehicle, the hour and the route are yours to choose.

From sunrise jeep runs to last-light boat rides, our team handles the route planning, the props and the timing, so you can just be present for it.

Three ways into the story

Each route is its own mood — pick one, or string two together for a fuller shoot.

Route One

Open Jeep Shoot

Dust trails, wide skies and a stripped-down jeep — built for big, cinematic frames against the Almatti backcountry.

Route Two

Boating Shoot

Still reservoir water, soft reflections and quiet drifting — for the slower, more intimate half of your story.

Route Three

Vintage Bike Shoot

A classic motorcycle, an empty road and that unmistakable old-world rhythm — retro, raw and a little rebellious.

Bonus Route

Scenic Locations

Hilltop overlooks, riverside trails and the reservoir's quieter corners — for couples who'd rather walk than ride.
